ClinicEMR

Free & Open Source EMR for Kenyan Healthcare

Simple. Open. Self-Hosted.

ClinicEMR is an open-source Electronic Medical Records system built for Kenyan clinics and hospitals. Designed for real clinical workflows, on-premise deployments, and full data ownership.

View on GitHub

Key Features

  • Patient registration and visit management
  • OPD, IP, and Emergency workflows
  • Billing, pharmacy, and inventory
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
  • Designed for Kenyan healthcare settings

Open Source

ClinicEMR is community-driven and free to use. You can deploy it, modify it, and contribute improvements.
